About Soghan

we turn ideas into works of art.

Soghan Contracting Co., L.L.C. was established in 1994 as a contracting firm with registered office in Dubai, UAE. We started our activities in general maintenance works which includes painting ,steel works, civil works. Our growth during the past sixteen(16) years has been steady and rapid. The achievement was made possible due to our proper planning, professional expertise and our relentless efforts to cope with international standards. We have established a reputable name in the Emirates for our service and quality work commensurate with International Standards through our extensive local experience in the construction industry.
